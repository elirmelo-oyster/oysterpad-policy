OysterPad Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

OysterPad is developed and published by Oyster Enterprises LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how OysterPad handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

OysterPad is designed to operate entirely on your device. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information such as your name, email address, location, contacts, audio recordings, or payment information.

OysterPad is a paid application distributed through Apple’s App Store. All purchases, billing, and account-related information are handled exclusively by Apple. OysterPad and Oyster Enterprises LLC do not have access to this information.

OysterPad does not use analytics services, tracking technologies, or user profiling.

2. Audio and Device Processing

All audio playback, looping, and sound processing are performed locally on your device. No audio data is transmitted or stored externally.

3. Internet Connectivity

OysterPad does not require internet access for its core functionality. Any future online features will be reflected in an updated version of this policy.

4. Third-Party Services

OysterPad does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

5. Data Sharing

Since no personal data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

OysterPad does not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Data Retention

No personal data is retained because none is collected.

8. Security

The app is designed to minimize privacy risks by keeping all processing on-device.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app evolves. Updates will be reflected with a new effective date.
